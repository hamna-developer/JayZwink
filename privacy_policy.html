Introduction

JayZwink ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and retain personal information when you visit our website or engage with our services.

Scope: This policy applies to personal data collected via our website, emails, forms, and client interactions. It does not apply to information collected offline or by third parties not acting on our behalf.

Scope

This policy covers:

  • Visitors to our website and users of our online tools.
  • Clients who communicate project requirements or make purchases.
  • Candidates who apply for jobs, contractors, and partners.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly and data collected automatically.

Categories:

  • Personal details: name, email, phone number, company name, billing address, job title.
  • Project details: scope, technical requirements, files, communication logs you share.
  • Payment info (when required): we may collect limited payment information via secure third-party payment processors — we do not store full card data on our systems.
  • Usage & technical data: IP address, device information, browser type, operating system, pages visited, referrer, timestamps, performance metrics.
  • Support communications: chat transcripts, emails, voice notes you send to us.

How we collect: directly from you (forms, emails), automatically via cookies and analytics, or from third parties (e.g., payment gateways, analytics providers).

How We Use Data

We process personal data to operate our business, provide services, and meet legal obligations.

  • Service delivery: to design, build, host, and maintain websites or applications you commission.
  • Communication: to respond to inquiries, provide quotes, schedule meetings, and send service-related updates.
  • Billing & payments: to manage invoices and payments (through secure third-party processors).
  • Improvements & analytics: to understand usage, diagnose issues, and improve our offerings.
  • Marketing (if opted in): to send newsletters or promotional offers when you subscribe. You can opt out anytime.
  • Legal compliance: to comply with laws, prevent fraud, and respond to legal requests.

Legal Basis for Processing

Where applicable (e.g., for residents of jurisdictions with data protection laws like GDPR), we rely on one or more lawful bases:

  • Contract: processing necessary to perform a contract with you (e.g., delivering a website you hired us to build).
  • Consent: where you have provided consent (e.g., marketing emails).
  • Legitimate interests: such as improving services, fraud prevention, or direct communications where balanced against your rights.
  • Legal obligation: to comply with laws or court orders.

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to provide core functionality and analytics.

Types of cookies

  • Essential: required for site functionality (forms, security).
  • Performance & analytics: to measure site usage (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Functional: remember preferences and settings.
  • Advertising: when used, to deliver relevant ads — we only use such services with consent where required by law.

You can manage cookie preferences in your browser. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality.

Third-Party Services

We may share personal data with trusted third-party providers who perform services on our behalf, including:

  • Hosting and infrastructure providers (e.g., Netlify)
  • Version control and collaboration platforms (e.g., GitHub)
  • Payment processors (e.g., Bank Transfer, Easypaisa, JazzCash, Wise, Payoneer, etc.)
  • Analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Support and communication platforms (email, chat)

Note: We require third parties to implement appropriate security measures and process data only on our instructions.

Data Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect personal data:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS).
  • Access controls and least-privilege access for staff.
  • Regular security testing and updates.
  • Incident response procedures for potential breaches.

Although we use reasonable measures, no system is 100% secure. If a breach occurs, we will follow applicable laws to notify affected individuals and authorities where required.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described or as required by law.

  • Transactional records: retained for accounting, tax, or legal obligations (typical retention: 6–7 years depending on jurisdiction).
  • Project data: retained while account is active and for a reasonable period after completion for support & maintenance.
  • Marketing records: until you unsubscribe or withdraw consent.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights including:

  • Access: request a copy of your data.
  • Rectification: correct inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: request erasure where lawful.
  • Restriction or objection to processing.
  • Data portability where applicable.

To exercise any right, contact us (see Contact section). We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling requests.

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13 (or greater age where required). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us to request deletion.

International Transfers

If we transfer personal data outside your country, we will implement appropriate safeguards (e.g., standard contractual clauses) to ensure adequate protection in accordance with applicable laws.

Payment Processing

We use trusted third-party payment providers to process transactions securely.

  • Payment methods: We accept Bank Transfer, Easypaisa, JazzCash, Wise (formerly TransferWise), and Payoneer.
  • Deposit requirements: For larger projects, we typically require a 50% deposit before work begins.
  • Balance payment: The remaining balance is due upon project completion and approval.
  • Security: All payment information is processed through secure, encrypted channels.
  • Data handling: We do not store full payment details on our servers.

Project Information & Estimation

How we handle project details and estimation processes.

  • Project estimation: We provide accurate quotes after reviewing your specific project requirements.
  • Requirements gathering: We collect detailed information about your project needs to determine scope, timeline, and final pricing.
  • Cost determination: Final project costs are determined based on the complexity, features, and timeline of your project.
  • Data usage: Project information is used solely for delivering the requested services and is kept confidential.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will post the updated policy with a revised "Last updated" date. For material changes, we may provide prominent notice (e.g., email or site banner).
