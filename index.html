Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our web development services and processes

How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline depends on the complexity and scope of your project. A basic website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while more complex e-commerce or custom web applications can take 8-12 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline after our initial consultation.

Do you provide website maintenance after launch?

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance plans that include regular updates, security monitoring, backups, and technical support. Our maintenance plans are customizable based on your specific needs and budget.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! All our websites are built with a mobile-first approach and are fully responsive. They will look great and function perfectly on all devices, from smartphones to desktops.

Can I update the website content myself?

Yes, we build custom websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript which gives you full ownership of your code. While these technologies require some technical knowledge to update, we provide comprehensive documentation and can implement a simple content management system or teach you how to make basic content changes yourself. For more complex updates, we offer affordable maintenance packages.

What is your pricing structure?

We offer both project-based pricing and hourly rates, depending on your needs. Our projects typically range from Rs. 1500 for basic sites to Rs. 100000+ for complex applications. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your specific requirements.

Do you provide hosting and domain services?

We can help you purchase and set up domain names and hosting. We partner with reliable hosting providers and can manage your hosting for you, or we can work with your existing hosting provider if you prefer.

Still have questions? We're here to help!
