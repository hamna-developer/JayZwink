Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical web development project take?

The timeline varies based on project complexity. A simple website typically takes 2-3 weeks, while more complex projects with custom functionality can take 6-8 weeks or longer. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do I need to purchase hosting separately?

We can either help you set up hosting with a provider of your choice, or we can manage hosting for you as part of our maintenance package. We recommend managed hosting for optimal performance and security.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! All websites we develop are fully responsive and optimized for all devices, including smartphones, tablets, and desktops. Mobile-friendliness is also a key factor in SEO rankings.

Can you work with my existing website?

Yes, we can enhance, redesign, or add new features to your existing website. We'll start with an assessment of your current site and provide recommendations for improvements.

What if I need changes after the website is launched?

We offer ongoing maintenance and support packages that include content updates, feature additions, and technical support. You can choose a plan that fits your needs.
